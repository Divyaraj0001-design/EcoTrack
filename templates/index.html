Frequently Asked Questions
EcoTrack uses internationally recognised emission factors (IPCC, Defra) to convert your transport distance, diet type, energy usage, and shopping activity into kg of CO₂ equivalent (CO₂e). Transport emissions are calculated per km by vehicle type; food by diet category × daily factor; energy from kWh and gas m³; shopping by spend level.
Your Carbon Score is a letter grade: Excellent (under 100 kg/mo), Good (100–200 kg), Average (200–333 kg), High (333–500 kg), Critical (over 500 kg). The global average is ~333 kg/month. Aim to get into "Good" or better! 🌟
Click Log Activity in the sidebar. Under Transport, choose your mode (Car, Flight, Bus) and enter your distance in km. You can also add start and end locations to visualise your route on the Eco Map.
Eco Challenges are daily and weekly tasks designed to help you reduce your footprint — like cycling instead of driving, or switching to a plant-based meal. Completing them earns points and unlocks achievement badges!
EcoBot is your AI carbon coach. Click the green robot icon in the bottom-right corner to open the chat. Ask it about your score, how to reduce emissions, challenge suggestions, or anything eco-related. It uses your real stats to give personalised advice.
The Eco Map shows your logged transport routes colour-coded by emission level (green = low, red = high). You can also find nearby eco-friendly places (bike rentals, EV charging, recycling centres) using the Green Spots layer, powered by free OpenStreetMap data.
Go to Import Data in the sidebar. Drag a CSV file with columns: date, category, activity, amount, unit — or download the sample CSV for the exact format. You'll see a preview before confirming. Duplicate rows are skipped automatically.
Your GPS location (Find Me button) is only stored in your browser's localStorage — never sent to our servers. Route coordinates you enter manually are saved to your Firestore account for map display only.
Go to Settings → Danger Zone and click "Delete Account". You'll be asked to type DELETE to confirm. This permanently removes your account and all data and cannot be undone.
EcoTrack is a Progressive Web App (PWA) — you can install it on your device from the browser menu. The dashboard shell loads offline, but activity logging and chart data require an internet connection to sync with Firebase.
